Diagnostics — Test My PC

Reads real specs from your browser (CPU cores, memory, GPU, screen, battery). Then runs a 5-second math + WebGL benchmark to score how fast your PC is.

Numbers shown are real measurements, not guesses.

Diagnostics — Test My Internet

Pings Dad's server (5 times, gives average latency). Then downloads a known-size payload and measures real bytes-per-second to calculate Mbps.

If speed is under 10 Mbps, Wi-Fi is probably weak. Move closer to the router or try a wired cable.
